    Pola’s name was derived from the Tagalog word for “red,” the color its soil. The town is now classified as a third class municipality and is composed of 23 barangays namely: Bayanan, Batuhan, Bacungan Buhay na Tubig, Biga, Bacawan, Casiligan, Calubasanhon, Calima, Campamento, Malibago, Misong, Matulatula, Maluanluan, Puting Cacao, Pula, Panikihan, Tagbakin, Tiguihan and Tagumpay. Two of these barangays are urban Zone I and Zone ii.
